Portfolio simulation game rules

Create an investment portfolio and watch it grow!

You will be given some starting funds (this money is fake and cannot be withdrawn) to invest in the financial market. Currently, this only includes equity investments (aka stocks). Fixed income assets such as bonds and derivatives such as options are not included (yet???). Also, no shorting. ETFs are allowed as long as it is traded on a stock market.

Track your performance over time and Buy/Sell your way to victory! Assert your dominance as a finance bro by out-performing other peoples portfolios!

To get started, just sign up and initiate your portfolio --> My Portfolio

Details:
- Start with ${{ starting_funds }} USD
- No comissions or fees when buying/selling
- No partial shares allowed
- Trades are assumed to be instant, and will be executed at quoted price.
- If buying/selling after market close, closing price will be used.
- All times displayed are in EST unless otherwise stated.

* Note currency conversion is still a work in progress. The default currency is USD, so if you buy any foreign stocks, there will be errors...
